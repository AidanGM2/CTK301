GAMES

Cat VS Fish

This is a game I worked on in 2023 for my game dev class. The game is a simple shmup where you play as a cat fighting evil fish. This game was also on display at the 2023 Games Showcase at ISU.

The Shining: The Game

This is a game I worked on in my sophomore year of high school. This was part of a book report project based on Steven King's "The Shining", in which we had to make an art piece related to our book. The game is a point and click inspired by classic LucasArts adventure games.
